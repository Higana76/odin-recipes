Pancakes

Description

Easy to make homemade fluffy pancakes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Mix together the flour, baking powder and white sugar.
  2. Separate 1 egg. Save egg whites in a different bowl.
  3. Mix egg yolk and dry ingredients. Slowly adding milk as needed.
  4. Let mix stand for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Whip whites.
  6. Fold egg whites into pancake mix.
  7. Add butter to a pan on low heat.
  8. Add pancake mix, flip when pancake starts to bubble.
  9. Adjust heat as necessary.
